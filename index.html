SafariPA Privacy Policy

Operated by StarKogi Tech And Automation  |  Effective: April 19, 2026

Last updated: April 19, 2026

SafariPA is an AI-powered assistant by StarKogi Tech And Automation that helps Safaricom customers purchase products and services via WhatsApp. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it.

1. Who We Are

SafariPA is operated by StarKogi Tech And Automation. We provide an AI-powered conversational assistant accessible via WhatsApp and other digital channels to help users interact with Safaricom products and services.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at the details provided in Section 10.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

Information you provide directly

  • Phone number (MSISDN) used to initiate the conversation
  • Recipient phone number when purchasing for someone else
  • Product or service selections made during a session
  • Payment method preference (M-Pesa, Airtime, Bonga Points, Okoa ETU)
  • Messages and queries submitted to the assistant

Information collected automatically

  • Session identifiers and timestamps
  • Channel type (WhatsApp, Web, USSD)
  • Interaction logs for quality and safety monitoring
  • Error and diagnostic data

Information we do NOT collect

  • M-Pesa PINs or payment credentials
  • National ID or passport numbers
  • Biometric data
  • Location data beyond what is inferred from phone number prefix

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Safaricom PLC — to fulfill your product or service order
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — as the AI model provider that processes your messages; Anthropic's data processing is governed by their own privacy policy
  • Meta Platforms (WhatsApp) — as the messaging channel; governed by Meta's privacy policy
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — for secure hosting and message routing (e.g. AWS)
  • Law enforcement or regulators — when required by applicable law

All third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements and are required to implement appropriate security measures.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data where no legal obligation requires retention
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details in Section 10. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Incident response procedures

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the relevant authorities and affected individuals as required by applicable law.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside Kenya, including in the United States (Anthropic, AWS) and Ireland (Meta/WhatsApp). Where data is transferred internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms recognised under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019.

9. Children's Privacy

SafariPA is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.
